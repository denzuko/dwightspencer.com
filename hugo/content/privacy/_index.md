## 2. What data is collected

### 2.1 Server and CDN logs

This site is served via Cloudflare. Cloudflare may log IP addresses, request
paths, timestamps, user-agent strings, and referrer headers as part of normal
CDN and DDoS-protection operation. These logs are held by Cloudflare under
their own [privacy policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).
I receive only aggregate traffic summaries.

No server-side analytics scripts are deployed by me. Cloudflare provides
aggregate analytics as a byproduct of CDN operation; a privacy-respecting
[Plausible Analytics](https://plausible.io) integration is planned as an
opt-in A/B test when it goes live this policy will be updated.

### 2.2 Contact

There is no contact form on this site. Inbound contact is via LinkedIn or
Keybase. I do not add you to any mailing list, share your information, or
retain correspondence beyond operational necessity.

### 2.3 Cookies

I set no first-party cookies. See the [Cookie Policy](/cookies/) for the
full inventory including the Cloudflare `__cf_bm` security cookie.

### 2.4 Third-party resources

Pages on this site load the following external resources:

- **Google Fonts** (fonts.googleapis.com) — Bricolage Grotesque typeface.
  Your IP is logged by Google when the font is fetched. See the
  [Google Fonts Privacy FAQ](https://developers.google.com/fonts/faq/privacy).
  Self-hosting is on the roadmap.
- **Stack Exchange** (stackexchange.com) — profile flair badge image on the
  homepage. Stack Exchange may set session cookies if you are logged into
  their network.
- **Avataaars** (avataaars.io) — avatar illustration. Generated server-side,
  no cookies.

No advertising networks, social media tracking pixels, or behavioral analytics
are intentionally loaded.

### 2.5 Analytics roadmap

[Plausible Analytics](https://plausible.io) — cookieless, no fingerprinting,
GDPR-compliant by design — is planned as an A/B test against Cloudflare
aggregate data. If and when enabled, this policy and the
[Cookie Policy](/cookies/) will be updated and a notice added to the site.

## 3. Legal bases (GDPR)

For EEA visitors:

- **Contract / pre-contractual** (Art. 6(1)(b)) — processing inbound contact

Under GDPR (EEA), NY SHIELD Act, and CCPA (California) you have rights
