## tl;dr

This site sets **no first-party cookies**. No consent banner is needed.
One Cloudflare security cookie may be set; it does not track you across sites.

---

## What is a cookie

A cookie is a small text file a website stores in your browser. This site
uses no login sessions, no advertising networks, and no tracking identifiers.

## Cookie inventory

| Name | Source | Purpose | Duration | Cross-site tracking |
|------|--------|---------|----------|-------------------|
| `__cf_bm` | Cloudflare | Bot mitigation — distinguishes human visitors from automated traffic | 30 minutes | No |

No other cookies originate from this site's code.

## Third-party requests that may set cookies

- **Google Fonts** — Google states it does not use font requests to build
  advertising profiles. See
  [Google Fonts Privacy FAQ](https://developers.google.com/fonts/faq/privacy).
- **Stack Exchange** — the profile flair image on the homepage is fetched
  from Stack Exchange servers. Stack Exchange may set session cookies if you
  are logged into their network. See
  [Stack Exchange Cookie Policy](https://stackoverflow.com/legal/cookie-policy).

## Future analytics

If [Plausible Analytics](https://plausible.io) is enabled it operates without
cookies, without fingerprinting, and without cross-site tracking. This policy
will be updated when that changes.

## Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking
`__cf_bm` may cause additional security checks but the site remains functional.
