## 2. Music licensing

Da Planet Radio maintains the following performance licenses for scheduled
broadcast:

| License | Scope |
|---------|-------|
| **ASCAP blanket license** | ASCAP-registered compositions and performances |
| **BMI blanket license** | BMI-registered compositions and performances |
| **SESAC blanket license** | SESAC-registered compositions and performances |

Broadcast is internet-only. Licenses are renewed annually. Current license
status is available on request via
[Keybase](https://keybase.io/Denzuko) (subject: `Radio License`).

### 2.1 Content outside blanket license scope

The following categories of content are played under separate rights
or by explicit permission:

- **Original works** by Dwight Spencer and Da Planet Security contributors —
  owned outright, no license required
- **Creative Commons licensed works** — played per the specific CC license
  terms; attribution metadata is included in Icecast stream tags where
  technically possible
- **Podsafe and royalty-free music** — played per the terms of the relevant
  podsafe catalog or individual artist permission
- **Hacker Public Radio (HPR) episodes** — played with attribution per the
  HPR license framework
- **aNONradio content** — played per standing coordination with aNONradio operators

### 2.2 Content not played

The following are not broadcast without a separately executed license:

- Synchronization-licensed content
- Works under active DMCA dispute
- Works from entities on US export control or sanctions lists

## 4. Asset submission

Third-party artists and producers may submit content for consideration.
Submission constitutes the following representations and warranties:

1. You are the copyright owner or hold a valid license to authorize broadcast
2. The work does not infringe any third-party intellectual property rights
3. The work does not contain content that is defamatory, obscene, or illegal
4. You grant Da Planet Security LLC a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to
   broadcast the submitted work on Da Planet Radio in perpetuity, revocable
   on 30 days written notice
5. You retain all other rights in the work

Submit via [Keybase](https://keybase.io/Denzuko) (subject: `Radio Submission`)
with the following:
- Artist name and contact information
- Track title, duration, and genre
- License terms (CC variant, podsafe catalog, or custom)
- Audio file (FLAC preferred; MP3 320kbps acceptable)

## 5. DMCA procedure for stream content

If you believe a broadcast asset on Da Planet Radio infringes your copyright,
follow the procedure in the [DMCA Policy](/dmca/). For stream-specific
notices, identify the asset by:

- **Broadcast timestamp** (UTC) and stream URL (`klaxon.dapla.net`)
- **Track title, artist, and rights holder**
- **Evidence** that the work is not covered by the ASCAP, BMI, or SESAC
  blanket licenses in force

Upon receipt of a complete stream-specific notice:

1. The identified asset will be removed from the Liquidsoap playlist
   within **24 hours** (faster than the general 14-day policy window,
   given the real-time nature of broadcast)
2. Icecast metadata logs for the relevant broadcast window will be
   preserved for the duration of the dispute
3. The submitter of the asset (if a third party) will be notified

Counter-notices follow the procedure in [DMCA Policy §4](/dmca/#4-counter-notice-17-usc--512g).

## 6. Listener rights

Listeners may:

- Tune in to the live stream for personal listening
- Record brief excerpts (under 3 minutes per broadcast session) for personal
  non-commercial use, subject to the rights of the individual track owners

Listeners may not:

- Rebroadcast the stream in whole or in part
- Record and redistribute full tracks or episodes
- Rip or capture audio for AI training, voice synthesis, or persona simulation
- Use the stream to circumvent the licensing framework (e.g., systematic
  recording of full albums as broadcast)
