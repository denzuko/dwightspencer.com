Privacy Policy

Effective: 2026-05-23 · Applies to: dwightaspencer.com

tl;dr

This site does not track you. No advertising. No behavioral profiling. No personal data sold or shared. If you email me, I read it and reply. That is the full extent of data processing here.

1. Who is responsible for this site

Dwight Spencer, operating as a private individual at dwightaspencer.com. Contact: see the security.txt for the current contact address. Mailing address available on request for legal correspondence only (NYS Address Confidentiality Program participant).

2. What data is collected and why

2.1 Server and CDN logs

This site is served via Cloudflare. Cloudflare may log IP addresses, request paths, timestamps, user-agent strings, and referrer headers as part of normal CDN and DDoS-protection operation. These logs are held by Cloudflare under their own privacy policy. I do not have real-time access to individual request logs; I receive only aggregate traffic summaries.

No server-side analytics scripts are deployed by me on this site. Any analytics scripts injected by Cloudflare at the edge are not at my direction and I am actively working to disable them.

2.2 Contact and email

If you contact me by email, I process your email address and message content solely to respond. I do not add you to any mailing list, share your address, or retain correspondence beyond what is operationally necessary. Email is stored on infrastructure I control or trust (dapla.net).

2.3 Cookies

I do not set any first-party cookies. No login sessions, no preferences, no tracking identifiers originate from this site's code. Cloudflare may set a short-lived security cookie (__cf_bm) for bot mitigation purposes — this is a technical necessity of CDN operation and does not identify you across sites or sessions.

2.4 Third-party resources

Pages on this site load the following external resources:

  • Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) — loads Bricolage Grotesque typeface. Google receives your IP address and browser headers when the font is fetched. This is a known tradeoff; a self-hosted font option is planned for a future revision.
  • Stack Exchange flair (stackexchange.com) — a profile badge image loaded on the homepage. Stack Exchange receives your IP address and browser headers when the image is fetched.
  • Buy Me a Coffee (images.squarespace-cdn.com) — a button image on the homepage. Loading this image sends your IP to Squarespace's CDN.
  • Avataaars (avataaars.io) — the avatar image. No cookies; image is generated server-side.

No advertising networks, social media tracking pixels, or behavioral analytics platforms are intentionally loaded by this site.

2.5 Future analytics

This site may in future use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-respecting, cookieless analytics tool that does not track individuals, does not use fingerprinting, and is GDPR-compliant by design. If and when this is enabled, this policy will be updated and a notice added to the site.
